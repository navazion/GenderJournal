HOW TO DECORATE A LITTLE GIRL'S ROOM

Inspired by painting my house.

“This color’s too pink,” I claimed and my brother smothered on more paint onto the walls.

“It’s what mom picked,” he said, stepping away from the wet wall to wipe his forehead. “And what does too pink mean?”

“It’s like the color of my room, baby pink,” I said, tilting my head slightly at the different sized rollers and paint brushes. “A little too pink for a breakfast room.”

“I don’t know what you’re talking about.”

“The ceiling looks like a boy’s room, too.”

“What’s wrong with a boy’s room?”

“Nothing, but it’s not a breakfast room.”

“Listen, mom picked these colors.”

“I know, I just think we should reconsider our choices before it’s too late.”

“It’s too late.”

“What makes you say that?”

“We already finished the ceiling, are you serious?”

“It can be fixed. Maybe we should paint it black. Or purple. Thoughts?”

“Let’s paint it in the colors we bought from Home Depot. Those are my thoughts.”

“Suit yourself, I suppose. But these walls are too pink.”

“TOO PINK??”

“Too pink.”

“You’re too picky.”

“And now you’re covered in baby blue and baby pink paint.”

“Great, now I’m too pink.”
