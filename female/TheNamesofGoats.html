THE NAMES OF GOATS

Inspired by a class discussion.


There are so many names
in this strange game
of trying to name
an animal.
From Ferdinand to Miranda,
Ariel to Billy
(Collins), it’s easy to see
why we couldn’t decide.
There were two sides:
To pun or not to pun?
I thought there was no fun
in a name without a joke.
But no one asked the goat.
We all tried to coat
the animal in whatever would fit.
We also considered: Emily Dickinson,
Goatzalo, Kurtz, and Gerald--
but what new name
will this toy goat herald,
even though we don’t know
if it’s a girl or a boy?
Does the goat have a name, even now?
We have yet to know.
