BABY ROOM PLANS.

Inspired by: painting my house.


So you’re planning a baby’s room. Lucky for you, we have a list of things you will and won’t need for their room.


THINGS YOU WILL NEED:
  1. A baby.
  2. A crib or containment device.
  3. A blanket or warming device.
  4. A listening device of some kind.
  5. Preferably a chair.
  6. 4 walls.
  7. 1 door.
    • 2 handles.
    • Door frame.
  8. 1 ceiling.
  9. 1 floor.
    • Planks of wood OR carpeting.
  10. At least 1 window.
  11. Glass.
  12. Screen.
  13. Sill.
  14. Music.
  15. Other pieces of furniture (optional).



THINGS YOU WON’T NEED:
  1. Spicy mexican salsa.
  2. Elephants.
  3. Novels.
  4. A life partner.
  5. A house that you own.
  6. Oranges.
  7. The unimaginable force of a black hole (you won’t need this directly).
